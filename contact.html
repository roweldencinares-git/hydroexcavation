Common Questions

How quickly can you start my project?

Our typical lead time is 3-7 days, but we can often accommodate urgent requests within 24-48 hours. Emergency services are available for utility strikes and critical situations.

Do you provide free quotes?

Yes! We provide detailed, no-obligation quotes based on your project scope. Most quotes are delivered within 24 hours of receiving your project details.

What information do you need for a quote?

To provide an accurate quote, we need: project location, type of excavation needed, estimated depth and length, timeline, and any access restrictions. The more details you provide, the more accurate our estimate.

Do you work with residential clients?

Our primary focus is commercial, industrial, and municipal projects. However, we can accommodate residential projects on a case-by-case basis, especially for larger excavation needs.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. BeachHydrovac is fully licensed, bonded, and insured for commercial excavation work in Virginia and surrounding states. Certificates of insurance are available upon request.
