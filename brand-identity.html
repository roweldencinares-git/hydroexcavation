BRAND STORY & POSITIONING

1.1 The "Local Edge" Strategy

While national competitors (like Badger) focus on massive, industrial "cold" branding, Beach Hydrovac is positioned as a local, veteran-owned alternative.

1.2 The Value Proposition

We combine the technical "brains" of AIM Locating (Advanced Infrastructure Mapping) with the specialized "muscle" of our new Hydrovac Truck.

We aren't just digging holes; we are protecting Virginia's underground infrastructure.

1.3 Target Audience

  • Primary:

    Electrical & Civil Contractors (who need precision trenching)

  • Secondary:

    Municipalities/HRSD (who require high-volume vacuum capacity)

TYPOGRAPHY RATIONALE

3.1 Heading: Inter (Black 900)

font-family: 'Inter', sans-serif;
BEACH

The Muscle

Used for "BEACH." It feels heavy, like the truck. It tells the contractor we have the power to handle Virginia clay.

The Modernity

We pair this with a lighter weight for "HYDROVAC" to show we are a high-tech division, not a legacy backhoe company.

3.2 Sub-Heading: Montserrat (Semi-Bold 700)

font-family: 'Montserrat', sans-serif;
A DIVISION OF AIM

The Precision

Used for "A DIVISION OF AIM." Its geometric, circular letters represent the surgical accuracy of our test holes.

3.3 Body: Roboto (Regular 400)

font-family: 'Roboto', sans-serif;
High-volume production capacity with 600ft hose reach. Mechanical boom precision for all-day site capacity. We protect Virginia's underground infrastructure with surgical accuracy.

The Functionalism

Used for service descriptions. It is clean and no-nonsense, signaling that we are easy to work with and data-driven.

PAGE 4

LOGO ARCHITECTURE

4.1 Design Logic

Minimalist Truck Boom

The logo features a minimalist truck boom silhouette that represents our mechanical precision.

The "Beach" Connection

Instead of a tidal wave (which looks destructive), we use a subtle curved horizon line in the "Warm Shoreline" (#D4A373) color.

The Veteran Badge

A small, discreet "Veteran Owned" seal is placed near the footer of the brand lockup to build immediate trust with government and local contractors.

Typography in Logo

BEACH (Inter Black - The Heavy Muscle)
HYDROVAC (Inter Regular - The Technical Process)

THE AIM CONNECTION

"Map First, Dig Second"

Partnership Strategy

The brand guidelines require that every piece of marketing mentions AIM Locating.

The Logic:

  • AIM finds the line
  • Beach Hydrovac digs it

This eliminates the "blame game" between the locator and the excavator.

Trust Partnership

AIM Locating + Beach Hydrovac = Complete Subsurface Solution

Brand Lockup Requirement

In the website footer and on truck doors, the AIM logo should appear alongside Beach Hydrovac as a "Trust Partner."
