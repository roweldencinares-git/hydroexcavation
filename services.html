Professional Hydro-Excavation Services

Safe, precise, and efficient excavation solutions for Virginia's infrastructure projects

Potholing / Daylighting

The cornerstone of safe excavation. We use high-pressure water and vacuum technology to safely expose underground utilities without damage—providing SUE Level A verification that engineers trust.

Why Choose Hydro-Excavation?

  • No Damage Risk: Water can't break pipes or cables
  • Precise Exposure: See exact depth, material, and condition
  • Documentation Ready: Photos for engineering records
  • Faster Than Digging: No waiting for hand excavation crews

Slot Trenching

Creating narrow, clean trenches for installing pipes, cables, or conduit. Hydro-excavation produces surgical precision with minimal soil disturbance and faster restoration.

Advantages of Hydro Slot Trenching

  • Minimal Backfill: Less material to remove and replace
  • Clean Edges: Straight, vertical walls for proper installation
  • Reduced Restoration: Less area to repave or landscape
  • Utility-Safe: Won't damage existing underground infrastructure

Remote Excavation (600ft Reach)

Our industrial vacuum truck features 600 feet of hose reach, enabling excavation in areas where traditional equipment can't go. Perfect for congested sites or restricted-access environments.

Ideal Applications

  • Behind Buildings: Access rear yards without driving through structures
  • Across Parking Lots: No need to close entire lots
  • Inside Facilities: Through doorways into interior spaces
  • Sensitive Landscaping: Preserve lawns, gardens, and hardscaping

SUE Level A Verification

The gold standard of subsurface utility mapping. We physically expose utilities to provide engineers with precise, verifiable data for design and construction planning.

What You Get

  • Exact Depth & Location: Measured to survey-grade accuracy
  • Material Identification: Steel, PVC, copper, fiber—confirmed visually
  • Condition Assessment: Corrosion, damage, or integrity issues noted
  • Photo Documentation: Visual proof for engineering records
