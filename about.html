Our Story

BeachHydrovac was founded with a simple mission: protect Virginia's underground infrastructure with the same dedication we brought to serving our country.

As a veteran-owned business, we understand the importance of precision, discipline, and attention to detail. These aren't just buzzwords—they're the foundation of everything we do. Every pothole we excavate, every utility we expose, and every project we complete is executed with military-grade standards.

Based in Virginia Beach and serving throughout Hampton Roads, we've built our reputation on reliability and excellence. Our clients—from municipal contractors to engineering firms—trust us because we show up on time, communicate clearly, and deliver results that exceed expectations.

A Division of AIM Locating

BeachHydrovac operates as a specialized division of AIM Locating (Advanced Infrastructure Mapping), one of Virginia's leading subsurface utility engineering firms.

This partnership creates a unique advantage: "Map First, Dig Second." When you hire BeachHydrovac, you're getting the combined expertise of locating specialists and excavation professionals working together seamlessly.
